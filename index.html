Negro Bills Canyon Trail

Negro Bills is about a 3 mile round trip hike in Moab, Utah. The trail follows the Colorado River which our dogs loved! Even in winter. There are many times you have to cross the river, but there are usually enough rocks you can step on to get across. The end of the hike features a beautiful arch and is well worth the trip.
This is a pretty easy hike that is good for all skill levels.

Trail Details
Distance: 3.2 miles
Time: 2 hours at a leisurely pace
Difficulty: Easy
